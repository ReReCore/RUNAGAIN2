Burpees
20 sec max reps
From standing, drop hands to floor, jump or step feet back to plank, chest to floor, push up, jump feet to hands, jump up with arms overhead. The single highest calorie-burning bodyweight move that exists. Go as fast as possible for the full 20 seconds.
💡 One burpee burns roughly the same calories as 10 sit-ups. 20 seconds of burpees triggers fat burning that continues for hours.
Jump squats
20 sec max reps
Squat down to parallel, then explode up into a jump, land softly and go straight into the next rep. Legs stay lean and long from jumping squats — it burns fat in the thighs without building bulk the way regular weighted squats do.
Mountain climbers
20 sec max reps

High knees — sprint pace
20 sec max reps
Same as warm-up but absolutely all out — knees to hip height as fast as humanly possible, pumping arms hard. This is running in place at sprint speed. The thighs lean out significantly from this done consistently.
Plank to downward dog
20 sec
From high plank, push hips up to downward dog, hold 1 sec, return to plank. Alternate rapidly. Works shoulders in a lengthening way — slims them, does not bulk.

60 sec only

Block 1 — Sun salutation flow (10 min · 5 rounds)
Continuous flow — no pausing between rounds. Builds internal heat and burns steadily. ~2 min per round, 5 rounds total.
① Mountain pose (Tadasana)
1 breath
Stand tall, feet together, arms by sides. Inhale deeply to set your intention and ground your posture.
② Forward fold (Uttanasana)
1 breath
Exhale and hinge forward from the hips — not the waist. Hands reach toward the floor. Head and neck fully release.
③ Half lift (Ardha Uttanasana)
1 breath
Inhale, lift your chest halfway — back flat and parallel to the floor, hands on shins or just hovering. Lengthen the spine fully before the next step.
④ Plank
Hold briefly
Step or jump both feet back into a high plank. Wrists directly under shoulders, body in one straight line from head to heels. Brace the core.
⑤ Chaturanga (slow lower)
3 sec down — this is everything
From plank, bend elbows and lower your body over a full 3 seconds — elbows track back along your ribs, not out. Stop when elbows hit 90°. The slow eccentric is what tones and slims the arms without adding bulk.
💡 The 3-second chaturanga lower creates more arm definition than any other bodyweight move. Never drop fast — you lose the entire benefit.
⑥ Upward dog (Urdhva Mukha)
1 breath
Inhale, press the tops of the feet into the floor, straighten arms and lift the chest and thighs off the mat. Shoulders roll back and down — chest opens fully.
⑦ Downward dog (Adho Mukha)
5 slow breaths
Exhale, tuck toes, push hips up and back into an inverted V. Press heels toward the floor. Keep the spine long. These 5 breaths are your active rest — use them.
⑧ Step / jump to front → Forward fold → Rise → Mountain
3 breaths
Step or lightly jump feet between hands, fold forward (exhale), then slowly roll up vertebra by vertebra to standing (inhale), arms sweep overhead, return to Mountain. One round complete — flow immediately into the next.

Non-negotiable rules
1
Vacuum exercise every single morning, empty stomach — 10 × 20 sec. Even rest days. This one habit changes the waist in 3 weeks.
2
6 training days, no exceptions. Missing one day per week = losing 8 full sessions across 8 weeks.
3
Sleep 7–9 hours minimum. Fat loss happens during sleep. Poor sleep stores belly fat regardless of training.
4
500ml water before every meal — lunch and dinner. Eliminates 1–2 cm of chronic bloat.
5
Measure every 2 weeks, not daily. Daily fluctuations of 1–2 kg are just water weight.
6
Warm up and cool down every session. Cold muscles tear instead of stretch — injury ends everything.
7
No comparing at week 2–3. Visible changes begin week 4. Trust the process in the uncomfortable early weeks.
8
No extra workouts on Sunday. Rest is structural — adding it increases cortisol and slows results.
Focus areas — how each works
Slim waist
Vacuum (daily) + pilates roll-ups + oblique criss-cross + reducing bloat foods + overall fat loss from cardio. The vacuum alone is transformative with daily practice.
Wider-looking hips
Fire hydrants + donkey kicks + hip thrusts + clamshells + deep hip stretches. Also: slimming the waist automatically makes hips look wider by ratio — the most powerful visual effect.
Slim arms
Overall fat loss (arms shed fast) + tricep dips + negative push-ups + arm circles. HIIT cardio and consistent arm toning work together for arm lean-out.
Slim inner thighs
Sumo pulses + inner thigh lifts + curtsy lunges + frog/butterfly/middle splits + overall cardio. Inner thighs respond visibly within 4–6 weeks.
Lean shoulders
Shoulder stretches post-workout + arm holds + chest opener posture + overall fat loss. Posture improvement alone changes how shoulders look dramatically — the chest opener is not optional.
